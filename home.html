Clandestine Crush
By: Wyeth V. Boquiron

This girl walked past your presence,
a silent harmony,
plays beneath her skin.
She has mastered the abstract of incuriosity,
keeping the feelings at bay.
Behind closed doors,
her feelings play.

In all these stories that await to unfold,
should forever be kept in place and remain untold,
there was only one audience to know,
this girl.
Be that as it may,
this girl’s feelings won’t linger around for long,
and soon will fade away.
A quieter sort of joy found in this young love,
solely are moments that paints the past.
A beautiful lasting memory of precious love

Thus, to you with no bit of understanding,
this clandestine crush,
that are sweet and rare,
offered to you,
are treasures found in whispered air.
The short joy is sure wonderful,
until it ruefully lasted.

Which there comes a day,
that feelings fade, enough to say,
“Thank you,”
“Next.”
